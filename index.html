Allama Muhammad Iqbal

1877-1938

The poet, philosopher, scholar and person who gave Urdu Poetry a new life

Sir Muhammad Iqbal (Urdu: محمد اقبال‎; 9 November 1877 – 21 April 1938), was a South Asian Muslim writer,philosopher, and politician, whose poetry in the Urdu language is among the greatest of the twentieth century, and whose vision of a cultural and political ideal for the Muslims of British-ruled India was to animate the impulse for Pakistan.
